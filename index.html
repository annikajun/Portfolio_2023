LearnBetter

"LearnBetter" is an app that functions as a Virtual Learning Environment to assist college students during remote studying. The app consists of eight features. Among other things, "LearnBetter" help students track their performance, lets them reach out and receive the required help, limits other phone activities, or provides a virtual group learning option.

Live Bus Timetable

This prototype was created a a final assignment in a Digital Fabrication and 3D Modelling class. The Bus Timetable was developed as a functional display by designing and 3D printing a custom casing and a vertical pole stand for an ESP32 and touchscreen. The device was programmed to fetch and display real-time bus arrival information.

Musicians Website

My website is about 5 Seconds of Summer, or 5SOS, an Australian pop-rock band. They formed in late 2011, and I have supported them since mid-2012. I always had a problem with their website, so I took an assignment from my module Introduction to Web Development as a chance to try and work out a better design for their site.
